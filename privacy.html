Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Amaravathi Public School, we recognize that privacy is important. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection and use of information provided via this website.

Information Collection

We may collect personal information such as student names, parent contact details, and addresses when knowingly provided through inquiry forms or admission applications.

Use of Information

Information collected is used strictly for:

  • Processing admission inquiries and applications.
  • Communicating school updates and academic progress.
  • Improving our website content and user experience.

Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure that personal information is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. We do not sell or share your data with third-party marketing agencies.
